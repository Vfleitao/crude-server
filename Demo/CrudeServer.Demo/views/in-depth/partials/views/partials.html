@@partial

Partials are exactly what the name says. Partial views that contain only a small part of your UI. Partials can contain other partials (this page is an example)
//page
@@layout in-depth/layout.html
<div class="h-100">
    @@partial in-depth/partials/views/intro.html
    <br />
    @@partial in-depth/partials/views/helpers.html
</div>

//partial in-depth/partials/views/helpers.html
